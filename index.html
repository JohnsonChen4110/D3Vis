  • From the color of each circle, we can get that the number of fatal collisions per billion miles has some geographical distribution patterns.
  • The southern area is majorly in red color, which indicates that the number of fatal collisions per billion miles in those states is above average. Meanwhile, Northeastern area is almost all green, which means they have below-average numbers of fatal collisions.
